John von Neumann

In this project, we will showcase the life and work of John von Neumann.

John von Neumann was born on December 28th 1903 in Budapest, Hungary and died on Febuary 8th 1957 in Washington, United States, at 53 years old. He was a hungarian-born mathematician. His father worked as a lawyer in Budapest, before becoming a legal advisor for a bank, later becoming a director. His mother stayed at home, and looked after the house and the children. After moving to the US, he officially changed his name from Neumann János to Johann von Neumann, and then to John von Neumann. He learned german and french at a young age, and later studied latin and ancient greek. He had two wives, Kövesi Marietta the first, whom he had a daughter with, and Dán Klára the second. Among other things, he mainly dealt with mathematics, phísics, and computer science.

John von Neumann vas an incredibly important mathematician, physicist, and computer scientist in the 20th century. He was born in hungary, and his work reached international levels of fame. Neumann’s work affected many fields, and is still important to this day. He is regarded as one of the founding fathers of computer science, and his memory is kept in the names of many institutions and prizes.
